Cacopsylla murrayi (Mathur, 1975), comb. nov.

Distribution. India (Hodkinson 1986, as Psylla murrayi): Meghalaya (Lahiri & Biswas 1979, 1980, 1990, as Psylla murrayi), Tamil Nadu (Kandasamy 1986, as Psylla murrayi), Uttarakhand (Mathur 1975, as Psylla murrayi).

Host plant. Murraya koenigii (Rutaceae).

Comments. Originally described in Psylla, this species is not congeneric with Psylla alni (Linnaeus, 1758), the type species of Psylla. For this reason, it is formally transferred here to Cacopsylla as C. murrayi (Mathur, 1975), comb. nov. from Psylla.
